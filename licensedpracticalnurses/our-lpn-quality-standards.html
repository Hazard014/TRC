Ensuring Quality LPN Candidates for Your Facility

Comprehensive screening and verification process to meet healthcare facility standards and regulatory requirements

Credential Verification

  • Provincial Registration: Verify active registration with BCCNM, CLPNA, or CNO
  • REx-PN / CPNRE: Confirm successful completion of registration examination
  • Education Validation: Verify diploma in practical nursing from accredited colleges
  • Continuing Education: Ensure ongoing certification and development

Clinical Competency Assessment

  • Experience Verification: Detailed review of clinical work history
  • Reference Checks: Contact past supervisors for performance feedback
  • Skill Assessment: Evaluate skills based on care setting needs
  • Specialty Certifications: Confirm BLS, wound care, med administration

Background and Compliance

  • Background Screening: Criminal record verification and compliance checks
  • Professional Standing: Confirm good standing and no disciplinary actions
  • Health Requirements: Ensure immunizations and health clearances
  • Documentation: Complete all records for facility compliance

Vendor Qualification Process

  • Facility Requirements: Collaborate to meet procurement qualifications
  • Insurance Documentation: Provide liability and malpractice coverage
  • Contract Compliance: Flexible contracts for your procurement needs
  • Quality Metrics: Establish metrics and performance reports
