Proven 7-Step Process for LPN Recruitment Success

Discovery & Assessment

We start by understanding your facility's current LPN needs, evaluating care requirements, reviewing patient acuity levels, assessing facility culture, and conducting stakeholder interviews to create a comprehensive foundation.

Strategy & Planning

Based on our discovery findings, we create a recruitment roadmap, prioritize candidate sources, choose channels, and set clear timelines for delivery and placement.

Sourcing & Screening

We actively source LPN candidates through our network, validate credentials and certifications, conduct initial screening interviews, and assess clinical competencies before presenting candidates to your facility.

Candidate Presentation & Interview

Our team presents qualified LPN candidates with full profiles, coordinates interviews, provides support, and facilitates the final selection to ensure the best match for your facility.

Verification & Compliance

We ensure full compliance through provincial registration checks, background screening, and credential validation before confirming LPN eligibility for deployment.

06

Onboarding & Integration

We assist with onboarding, documentation, and facility orientation to ensure your LPN integrates seamlessly into your care team.

Follow-up & Support

We stay involved post-placement with regular check-ins, performance monitoring, retention support, and future recruitment planning to ensure long-term staffing success.
