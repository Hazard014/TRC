How We Find the Right Nurse Practitioners for Your Practice

Specialized recruitment strategy targeting qualified advanced practice nurses across multiple specialty areas

1

New NP Graduates

Target: Recent Master's graduates from NP programs seeking first advanced practice positions

Sources: UBC, Trinity Western, University of Northern BC, and other accredited NP programs

Benefits: Current knowledge, fresh training, enthusiasm for advanced practice, technology proficiency

2

Experienced Advanced Practice Nurses

Target: Established NPs seeking new practice opportunities, specialty changes, or geographic relocation

Sources: Current NPs in other practices, specialty transitions, practice model changes

Benefits: Proven experience, established patient care skills, practice efficiency, mentorship capabilities

3

Specialty Practice Transitions

Target: NPs seeking to transition between specialty areas or expand practice scope

Sources: Family NPs moving to specialty practice, specialty NPs seeking broader scope

Benefits: Advanced practice experience, adaptability, specialty knowledge, scope expansion

4

Physician-Collaborative Seekers

Target: NPs seeking collaborative practice models with physician partnerships

Sources: Independent practitioners seeking collaboration, hospital NPs wanting clinic practice

Benefits: Collaborative mindset, interprofessional skills, practice model flexibility

5

Independent Practice Entrepreneurs

Target: Experienced NPs interested in independent or semi-independent practice opportunities

Sources: Business-minded NPs, entrepreneurial advanced practice nurses, leadership-oriented professionals

Benefits: Leadership skills, business acumen, patient care innovation, practice development

6

Rural and Remote Specialists

Target: NPs with experience or interest in rural and remote healthcare delivery

Sources: Rural health specialists, community health NPs, northern practice professionals

Benefits: Rural expertise, independence, community integration, comprehensive care skills
