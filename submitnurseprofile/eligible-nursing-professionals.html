Open to All Licensed Nursing Professionals

We welcome applications from all nursing specialties and experience levels

Registered Nurses (RN)

  • Requirements: Active BCCNM registration, BSN degree, NCLEX-RN certification
  • Opportunities: Hospital units, ICU, emergency departments, medical-surgical, specialty care
  • Experience: New graduates to experienced professionals welcome

Licensed Practical Nurses (LPN)

  • Requirements: Active provincial registration, nursing diploma, REx-PN or CPNRE certification
  • Opportunities: Long-term care, chronic care, rehabilitation, community health
  • Experience: All experience levels from new graduates to specialists

Travel Nurses

  • Requirements: Any nursing license (RN/LPN/NP), willingness to travel, housing flexibility
  • Opportunities: Short-term assignments, rural placements, emergency coverage
  • Experience: Experienced nurses with adaptability and independence

Psychiatric Nurses (RPN)

  • Requirements: RPNCE certification, psychiatric nursing education, western province registration
  • Opportunities: Psychiatric hospitals, addiction treatment, forensic settings, community mental health
  • Experience: Mental health specialists and crisis intervention experts
