Your Application Journey

What to expect after submitting your nursing profile

Within 24 Hours
  • Confirmation: Application received confirmation email
  • Initial Review: Basic qualification and completeness check
  • Follow-up: Request for any missing information or documents
Within 1 Week
  • Credential Verification: License and certification verification process
  • Reference Outreach: Initial contact with provided professional references
  • Screening Interview: Phone or video interview scheduling
Within 2 Weeks
  • Profile Completion: Full profile review and network registration decision
  • Network Welcome: Welcome package and opportunity matching begins
  • First Opportunities: Notification of matching opportunities
Ongoing
  • Opportunity Alerts: Regular notifications of new matching positions
  • Profile Updates: Ability to update preferences and qualifications
  • Professional Support: Ongoing career guidance and opportunity support
