Ensuring Quality RN Candidates for Your Facility

Comprehensive screening and verification process to meet healthcare facility standards and regulatory requirements

Credential Verification

  • BCCNM Registration: Verify active registration with BC College of Nurses and Midwives for all BC placements
  • NCLEX-RN Certification: Confirm successful completion of National Council Licensure Examination
  • Education Validation: Verify BSN degree from accredited nursing programs
  • Continuing Education: Ensure current professional development and certification maintenance

Clinical Competency Assessment

  • Experience Verification: Validate clinical experience through detailed work history review
  • Reference Checks: Contact previous supervisors and colleagues for performance verification
  • Skill Assessment: Evaluate clinical skills relevant to specific unit requirements
  • Specialty Certifications: Confirm unit-specific certifications (ACLS, BLS, specialty training)

Background and Compliance

  • Background Screening: Comprehensive background checks including criminal record verification
  • Professional Standing: Verify good standing with regulatory bodies and no disciplinary actions
  • Health Requirements: Ensure current immunizations and health clearances meet facility standards
  • Documentation: Complete personnel files with all required documentation for facility compliance

Vendor Qualification Process

  • Facility Requirements: Work with your procurement team to meet vendor qualification standards
  • Insurance Documentation: Provide required professional liability and general liability coverage
  • Contract Compliance: Flexible contract terms to meet your facility's procurement requirements
  • Quality Metrics: Establish performance metrics and reporting for successful partnerships
