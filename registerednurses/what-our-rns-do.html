Comprehensive Care for Complex and Unstable Patients

Our registered nurses hold Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSN) degrees and provide the highest level of nursing care. They work independently and as team leaders in the most demanding healthcare environments across BC.

Health Assessment & Care Planning
Complete patient assessments, develop comprehensive care plans, monitor patient progress, and adjust treatments based on clinical judgment.
Medication Administration & IV Therapy
Administer complex medications, manage IV therapy, titrate drips, and provide specialized medication management for critical patients.
Complex Patient Care
Care for unstable patients in ICU, emergency departments, step-down units, and specialized medical-surgical environments.
Health Promotion & Disease Prevention
Patient education, discharge planning, preventive care protocols, and community health initiatives.
Leadership & Patient Advocacy
Charge nurse responsibilities, team coordination, patient advocacy, and mentoring of junior nursing staff.
Work Environments
Our RNs are authorized to work in hospitals, community health centers, long-term care facilities, schools, and specialized healthcare settings across all BC health authorities.
