How We Find the Right RNs for Your Facility

Comprehensive recruitment strategy targeting qualified RN candidates across multiple channels and networks

1

New Graduate Programs

Target: Recent BSN graduates from BC nursing schools seeking first positions

Sources: UBC, BCIT, Thompson Rivers, University of Northern BC, Douglas College, Langara College

Benefits: Fresh knowledge, eager to learn, long-term potential, technology-savvy

2

Interprovincial Recruitment

Target: Experienced RNs from other provinces seeking BC opportunities

Sources: Alberta, Ontario, Saskatchewan healthcare professionals looking for lifestyle change

Benefits: Immediate experience, diverse backgrounds, proven track records

3

International Recruitment

Target: Qualified international RNs seeking Canadian opportunities

Sources: Philippines, India, UK, Australia, USA nurses with expedited pathways

Benefits: Global experience, cultural diversity, strong work ethic, specialized skills

4

Returning Nurses

Target: Experienced RNs returning to practice after career breaks

Sources: Nurses who left during pandemic, career gap professionals, life transition returners

Benefits: Proven experience, mature perspective, flexible scheduling availability

5

Career Advancement Seekers

Target: RNs seeking new challenges, specializations, or leadership opportunities

Sources: Current healthcare professionals looking for growth, specialty changes, location preferences

Benefits: Motivated professionals, proven performance, ready for new challenges
