Proven 7-Step Process for RN Recruitment Success

Our structured methodology ensures successful RN placement with qualified candidates and minimal risk.

01. Discovery & Assessment

Evaluate RN needs, patient acuity, infrastructure, and team input to define scope.

02. Strategy & Planning

Build roadmap, source strategy, recruitment timeline, and candidate profiles.

03. Sourcing & Screening

Source, screen, validate credentials, and assess clinical competencies.

04. Candidate Presentation & Interview

Present candidates, coordinate interviews, and support selection.

05. Verification & Compliance

Confirm BCCNM registration, run checks, and prepare documentation.

06. Onboarding & Orientation

Deliver training materials, assist with orientation, and ensure readiness.

07. Post-Placement Support

Monitor placement success, address feedback, and support future staffing needs.
