All Our RNs Meet BC's Highest Standards

Every registered nurse in our network holds proper education, licensing, and clinical experience

Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSN)

4-year degree required in all provinces except Quebec. Our RNs have completed comprehensive nursing programs from accredited institutions.

Key Components: Anatomy & physiology • Pharmacology • Pathophysiology • Clinical rotations • Evidence-based practice • Leadership training

Quebec DEC-BAC Stream

For Quebec-trained nurses: Diplôme d'études collégiales (DEC) in nursing plus university bridging program completion.

Recognition: Full BC practice recognition after BCCNM registration process

NCLEX-RN Examination

All our RNs (except Quebec-trained) have passed the National Council Licensure Examination for Registered Nurses.

Validation: Computer adaptive test ensuring competency in safe, effective nursing practice

BCCNM Registration

Active registration with BC College of Nurses and Midwives (BCCNM) — mandatory for all RN practice in BC.

Verification: Current registration status verified before each placement

Regional Variations Covered

  • Quebec Nurses: OIIQ exam and education model recognized
  • Northern Territories: Following provincial partner standards
  • Interprovincial Transfers: Registration facilitated and verified
