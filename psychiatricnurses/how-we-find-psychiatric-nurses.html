Our 7-Step Psychiatric RPN Recruitment Process

From discovery to post-placement support, every step ensures mental health staffing success.

01. Discovery & Assessment

Understand your psychiatric care needs, risks, modalities, and team environment.

02. Strategy & Planning

Define your mental health recruitment plan, timeline, and candidate profile targets.

03. Sourcing & Screening

Source RPNCE-certified nurses, conduct screening, assess therapeutic capabilities.

04. Interviews & Matching

Present top candidates, coordinate interviews, and ensure cultural and skill fit.

05. Verification & Compliance

Verify credentials, check licensing and mental health-specific compliance.

06. Integration & Orientation

Onboard your RPNs with mental health-specific documentation and training support.

07. Ongoing Support

Monitor placement success, provide ongoing support, and plan future needs.
