Specialized Education and Certification for Psychiatric Nursing

Diploma or Bachelor's degree programs with specialized mental health focus and provincial certification.

  • Specialized Training: Crisis response, de-escalation, therapeutic interventions

Mental Health Specialization

  • Core Subjects: Abnormal psychology, psychopharmacology, ethics, mental health law
  • Practical Skills: Assessment, intervention, documentation, safety protocols
  • Population Focus: Adult, geriatric, adolescent, addiction mental health

Licensing Requirements

  • RPNCE Certification: Registered Psychiatric Nurses of Canada Examination
  • Content Areas: Mental health assessment, crisis response, medication management
  • Provincial Registration: BC (CPNBC), Alberta, Saskatchewan, Manitoba
  • Continuing Competence: Ongoing education & license renewal requirements

Important: Psychiatric nursing is regulated only in western provinces. In other provinces, RNs or NPs with mental health specializations provide similar care.
