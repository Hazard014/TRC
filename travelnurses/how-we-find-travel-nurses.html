Proven 7-Step Process for Travel Nurse Recruitment

Our structured methodology ensures successful travel nurse placement with qualified candidates and minimal risk

1

Discovery & Assessment

Description: We start by understanding your facility's temporary staffing needs, assignment duration, specific requirements, housing arrangements, and urgency level to create a comprehensive foundation for travel nurse recruitment.

Deliverables: Temporary staffing analysis • Assignment requirement evaluation • Housing needs assessment • Timeline evaluation • Urgency level determination

Timeline: 1–2 weeks

2

Strategy & Planning

Description: Based on our discovery findings, we develop a comprehensive travel nurse recruitment strategy, prioritize candidate sources, plan housing logistics, create assignment timelines and provide realistic placement projections.

Deliverables: Travel nurse recruitment strategy • Candidate source prioritization • Housing coordination plan • Assignment timeline planning • Placement projections

Timeline: 1–2 weeks

3

Sourcing & Screening

Description: We actively source travel nurse candidates through specialized networks, validate credentials and provincial registration, conduct screening interviews, and assess adaptability for temporary assignments.

Deliverables: Specialized candidate sourcing • Credential validation • Provincial registration verification • Adaptability assessment • Assignment readiness evaluation

Timeline: 2–4 weeks

4

Candidate Presentation & Interview

Description: Our team presents qualified travel nurse candidates with complete profiles, coordinates interview schedules, provides assignment briefings, and facilitates the selection process for optimal candidate-assignment matching.

Deliverables: Qualified candidate presentation • Interview coordination • Assignment briefing • Selection facilitation • Reference verification

Timeline: 1–2 weeks

5

Registration & Compliance

Description: Comprehensive support for provincial registration processes, credential verification, background screening, and compliance documentation specific to travel nursing requirements.

Deliverables: Provincial registration support • Credential verification • Background screening • Compliance documentation • Assignment eligibility confirmation

Timeline: 2–4 weeks

6

Assignment Coordination & Support

Description: We coordinate assignment logistics including housing arrangements, travel planning, orientation scheduling, documentation completion, and integration support for successful assignment start.

Deliverables: Housing coordination • Travel arrangements • Orientation scheduling • Documentation completion • Integration support

Timeline: 1–2 weeks

7

Ongoing Assignment Support

Description: Continuous support throughout the assignment including performance monitoring, issue resolution, housing support, assignment extension discussions, and future placement planning.

Deliverables: Performance monitoring • Issue resolution • Housing support • Extension planning • Future assignment coordination

Timeline: Throughout assignment duration
