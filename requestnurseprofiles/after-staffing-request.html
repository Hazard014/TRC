Your Recruitment Journey

What to expect after submitting your nursing staffing request

Within 24 Hours
  • Confirmation: Request received confirmation email with reference number
  • Initial Review: Basic qualification and requirement assessment
  • Contact Scheduling: Phone consultation appointment scheduling
Within 48 Hours
  • Professional Consultation: Detailed discussion of your requirements and recruitment strategy
  • Proposal Development: Customized recruitment proposal and timeline
  • Vendor Information: Discussion of contract terms and vendor arrangements
Within 1 Week
  • Strategy Finalization: Approved recruitment strategy and candidate sourcing plan
  • Process Launch: Active candidate sourcing and recruitment execution
  • Regular Updates: Scheduled progress updates and communication plan
Ongoing
  • Candidate Presentations: Qualified candidate profiles and interview coordination
  • Interview Support: Interview scheduling and candidate management
  • Placement Assistance: Offer negotiation and onboarding support
