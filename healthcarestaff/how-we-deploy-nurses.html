Professional Healthcare Recruitment Process

Proven methodology for finding qualified nursing professionals to meet your facility needs

Step 1: Needs Assessment

Comprehensive evaluation of your facility's nursing requirements, including positions needed, qualifications required, and timeline expectations.

  • Staffing needs analysis
  • Position requirements
  • Qualification specifications
  • Timeline planning

Timeline: 1–2 weeks

Step 2: Recruitment Strategy

Develop targeted recruitment approach based on your specific needs, including candidate sourcing channels and screening criteria.

  • Recruitment plan
  • Sourcing strategy
  • Screening criteria
  • Quality standards

Timeline: 1–2 weeks

Step 3: Candidate Sourcing

Active recruitment through multiple channels including nursing schools, professional networks, and specialized healthcare recruiting.

  • Active candidate sourcing
  • Initial screening
  • Qualification verification
  • Interest assessment

Timeline: 2–4 weeks
